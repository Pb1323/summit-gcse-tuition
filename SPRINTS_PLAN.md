# SPRINTS PLAN

GradeSprint focuses on GCSE Maths and Science mocks, diagnostics, weak-topic analytics, sprint courses and tuition conversion.

- Premium UK education design with navy, royal blue, champagne and clean report cards.
- Local prototype data, no auth, payment or database.
- Mock room supports timer, question navigation, answer saving, flagging and review flow.
- Reports show score, estimated grade band, topic weaknesses, marks lost, careless mistakes and 7-day improvement plan.
- Admin tools provide question filtering and mock generation validation.

Next improvements: real persistence, richer marking, account history, tutor dashboard, PDF export and payments.
